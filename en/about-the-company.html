ABOUT THE COMPANY

Super Foods is the trade name of a company with main activities in the wholesale trade, distribution, import and export of meat, poultry, fish and other various food products.


Super Foods product range comprises of:

  • fresh chilled and frozen beef
  • mutton,
  • pork
  • poultry (chicken and turkey)
  • fish products 
  • by-products 


We source these from more than 400 producers and exporters to our global customer base comprising over 1000 companies internationally, including manufacturers, importers, distributors, wholesalers and retailers.


The company buys products from Europe, North and South America, and sells to its main markets in Eastern Europe.
