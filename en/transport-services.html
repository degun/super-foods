Transport services

Besides our rich trading experience we have professional knowledge in logistics. We take pride in being able to source all types of products from any point of the world and deliver it to any place in the world as per our clients’ request (via break bulk vessels, trucks, railway sections or containers).

The company operates international storage, sorting and packing hubs located in the ports of Klaipeda (Lithuania) and Poti (Georgia), thus having the option to deliver the goods we sources to any point in the European Union, Ukraine, Russia and the Customs Union and CIS countries (Tajikistan, Turkmenistan, Armenia, Georgia, Kyrgyzstan, Kazakhstan, Azerbaijan, Uzbekistan), as well as to markets in the Middle East, Far East, Australia, Africa etc.

Our Bulgarian company operates 8 cold stores in the biggest cities of the country. The company also boasts of a fleet of refrigerated trucks.

Benefiting from the effective operations strategy, improved management and well organized structure, global knowledge and regional expertise of its traders, MEAT TEAM boasts a consistent growth in the business. During the years the group expanded rapidly, increasing its turnover 10 fold, reaching more than 300 million US$ in 2012.

MEAT TEAM grows more competitive in the world meat market with every passing year , based on lower costs, better reliability and quality over the competition, combined with fast responsiveness and care to all of our customers and their unique/specific needs.
