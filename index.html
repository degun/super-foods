Super Foods

Super Foods is the trade name of a company with main activities in the wholesale trade, distribution, import and export of meat, fish and other various food products.

Products

The company buys products from Europe, North and South America, and sells to its main markets in Eastern Europe.

Logistics

Besides our rich trading experience we have professional knowledge in logistics. We take pride in being able to source all types of products from any point of the world and deliver it to any place in the world as per our clients’ request (via break bulk vessels, trucks, railway sections or containers).
